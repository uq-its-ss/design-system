# How to add templates into Vision 6

Instructions on how to add email templates into your vision 6 account.

## Create a new template in Vision 6 

1. Open your Vision 6
2. Click Messages in left menu
3. Click New message
4. Click More options > Use HTML
5. In the WYSWYG editor, click the HTML button in the control panel (highlighted in red)
6. Copy and paste one of the Vision 6 templates below into the HTML editor of Vision 6:
   1. Standard
7. Click Update
8. Click the dropdown arrow next to the Save button. Click Save as template and give the template an appropriate name.
9. On the next screen where it asks whether you wish to “clean” the message, select Don’t clean and click Continue.

This template can now be selected when creating a new message.

## Update footer content

1. Once a new message has been saved, the correct footer information must be added.
2. To do this:
   1. Click Messages in left menu
   2. Find your message in the list and click the 3-dot icon on the far right. Then click Footer details.
   3. Copy and paste the HTML footer template below into the Sender contact details area (highlighted in red) of the Footer details panel. Click **Save changes**.
