## Overview

*   The Previous-next component is also a form of pagination.
*   It provides previous and next buttons (links) to navigate between pages or between tab contents on the same page.
*   They are aligned to the sides of the page or in some instances to the sides of the tab contents.

## How and when to use

*   The Previous-next component can be used with or without the progression component.

### Breakpoints

*   The previous and next buttons collapses at viewport widths less than 640px.

## Related

*   Progression component could be used in conjunction with the Previous-next component.
